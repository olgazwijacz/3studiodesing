Jonh Deanwood

Bsc (Hons) BArch RIBA

John studied architecture at the University of Liverpool (BA Hons, B.Arch) during which time he also worked as an Architectural Assistant at Breslin Carr Associates in Ireland. ollowing completion of his studies, John spent five years working for Michael Wilfords practice Wilford Schupp Architekten in Stuttgart, Germany on a wide range of competitions and live projects at all work stages. These projects varied in size from one off houses and residential developments in Germany, office buildings in Russia and Australia to factory campuses in Malaysia. While at Wilford Schupp Architekten John gained his professional certification from the Architektenkammer Baden-Wurttemberg.

John then moved onto Hopkins Architects where he became Project Architect and, amongst other projects, was responsible for helping win a national competition for a new Research and Education Building for Alder Hey Children’s Hospital and delivered the construction of phase 1 of 2.
